About BlueMoon

Where Fashion Meets Function — connecting you with the best tailors, dry cleaners, and fabric vendors across Nigeria.

Our Story

BlueMoon was born out of a simple observation: finding reliable tailors, quality dry cleaners, and premium fabrics in Nigeria shouldn't be this hard. We saw countless people struggling with unreliable service providers, inconsistent quality, and a lack of transparency in the fashion and garment care industry.

We're building a platform that solves these problems by creating a trusted marketplace where service providers are verified, quality is guaranteed, and everything you need for your wardrobe is just a tap away.

What We Do

BlueMoon is a comprehensive fashion and garment care platform that brings together four key services:

  • Verified Tailors — Browse profiles, view portfolios, read reviews, and book appointments with skilled tailors who have been vetted for quality and reliability.
  • On-Demand Dry Cleaning — Schedule pickups and deliveries for your laundry and dry cleaning needs. Professional care for your garments, delivered to your doorstep.
  • Premium Fabrics — Discover and shop for high-quality fabrics from trusted vendors. From Ankara to lace, find the perfect material for your next outfit.
  • Ready-Made Outfits — Browse and purchase ready-to-wear fashion from talented designers. Quality fashion, ready when you are.

Our Mission

To become Nigeria's most trusted platform for fashion services and garment care, empowering local artisans and service providers while delivering exceptional experiences to our customers.

Why Choose BlueMoon?

  • Verified Providers — Every tailor, designer, and vendor on our platform goes through a verification process to ensure quality and reliability.
  • Transparent Pricing — No hidden charges. See prices upfront before you commit to any service.
  • Secure Payments — Your transactions are protected with industry-standard security measures.
  • Customer Support — Our dedicated team is always ready to help resolve any issues and ensure your satisfaction.
  • Referral Rewards — Earn up to ₦3,000 for every friend you refer to BlueMoon. Your friends get ₦500 bonus too!
